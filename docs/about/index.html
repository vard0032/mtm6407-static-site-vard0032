About Me

Hi, I’m Sophia — a creative designer and content creator with a passion for visual storytelling. I specialize in photography, branding, and digital media, helping bring ideas to life through clean and engaging design.

I’m currently studying Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College, where I’ve developed skills in web design, content creation, and user-focused design. I love creating work that not only looks good but also connects with people.

Whether it’s building a brand, capturing a moment, or designing a website, I’m always focused on creating meaningful and visually impactful experiences.
